# BIZBRIDGE

BIZBRIDGE is a smart B2B support platform designed to help MSMEs, rural entrepreneurs, and small business starters get easier access to raw materials, micro-loan awareness, and direct customer connectivity without depending on middlemen.

The project focuses on making early-stage business growth more accessible, affordable, and practical.

---

## Project Overview

Many small business owners and rural entrepreneurs face common challenges such as:

- difficulty in finding affordable materials,
- lack of proper financial support,
- limited customer reach,
- and dependency on middlemen for selling products.

BIZBRIDGE was created as a solution to bridge this gap by combining business support, direct trade opportunities, and financial accessibility in one platform.

---

## Key Features

- MSME starter support
- Raw material accessibility
- Micro-loan awareness
- Direct customer-to-seller connection
- Reduced middlemen dependency
- Easy business growth assistance
